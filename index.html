What do we offer?


Here at Fitness Formula we offer workout plans for any goal.
Wether you are looking to improve your strength, gain muscle size, lose fat or condition your body for a sport - we have a Fitness Formula for you!
Here at Fitness Formula, we like to keep it simple. Workouts don't have to be grueling and long, they can be fun and quick!
If there isn't a workout plan for you in our workout library, contact us and we would love to form a workout specialised just for you.


Strength Training


Strength training involves the performance of physical exercises that are designed to improve strength over time.


What our strength plans will look like:


  • 1 - 3 reps
  • 80% - 100% of your one rep max
  • 1:30 - 3 minutes of rest between sets

Build Muscle Size


is an increase and growth of muscle cells. Hypertrophy refers to an increase in muscular size achieved through exercise.


What our muscle size plans look like:


  • 6 - 12 reps
  • 30% - 60% of your one rep max
  • 0 - 1:30 minutes of rest between sets

Lose Fat


Fat loss refers to weight loss from fat through eating at a caloric defecit and if needed, cardio.


What our fat loss plans look like:


  • 0 - 60 minutes of cardio
  • Hypertrophy lifting
  • Eating at a caloric defecit
