What do we offer?


Here at Fitness Formula we offer workout plans for any goal.
Wether you are looking to improve your strength, gain muscle size, lose fat or condition your body for a sport - we have a Fitness Formula for you!
Here at Fitness Formula, we like to keep it simple. Workouts don't have to be grueling and long, they can be fun and quick!
If there isn't a workout plan for you in our exercises library, contact us and we would love to form a workout specialised just for you.


Strength Training

What is it?

Strength training involves the performance of physical exercises that are designed to improve strength over time.

What our strength plans will look like -

Using 80% - 100% of your one rep max

1:30 - 3 minute breaks between sets

1 - 3 reps per set
