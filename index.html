Strength Training


Strength training increases your strength over time through lifting heavy for fewer repitiions and mor rest.


What our strength plans will look like:


  • 1 - 3 reps
  • 80% - 100% of your one rep max
  • 1:30 - 3 minutes of rest between sets

Build Muscle Size


Putting your muscles under tension for a longer period of time increases your muscle size - this is called hypertrophy.


What our muscle size plans look like:


  • 6 - 12 reps
  • 30% - 60% of your one rep max
  • 0 - 1:30 minutes of rest between sets

Lose Fat


Fat lose is achieved through maintaining a caloric deficit and/or cardiovascular exercises.


What our fat loss plans look like:


  • 0 - 30 minutes of cardio
  • Hypertrophy lifting
  • Eating at a caloric defecit
